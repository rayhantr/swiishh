HOW TO PLAY

🖐 Camera mode

  1. Show your hand — palm toward the camera, whole hand in frame.
  2. Pinch (thumb + index) or make a fist to grab the ball.
  3. Move your hand to aim — the ball follows.
  4. Flick up and open your hand to shoot. Flick speed sets power; a higher arc is more forgiving.

Sit about an arm's length from the camera in decent light. If tracking is jumpy, slow down — the grab has hysteresis and won't drop the ball from jitter.

👆 Mouse & touch

  1. Press on the ball to grab it.
  2. Drag to aim, then flick upward and release to shoot.

🏀 The shot

Scoring: +3 for a swish, +2 off the rim or glass. The ball has real drag and backspin — the Magnus effect lifts a well-spun shot, and soft arcs die on the front iron. Aim assist (toggle in the menu) only trims forward distance, never your arc or aim.
